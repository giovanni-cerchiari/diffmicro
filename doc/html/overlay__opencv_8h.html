◆ overlay_type_opencv

enum overlay_type_opencv

Overlays are thought to be things drawn on top of the window_display_opencv They are not intented to modify the image but just lay on top of it. They can be anthing implemented through the virtual functions. For further manipulation the possibility to select and deselect the overaly is found to be important.

WARNING: the position for drawing inside may be intended in absolute frame. The absolute frame is define by the property of image in window_display_opencv
